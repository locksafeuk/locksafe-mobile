=== GUIDELINE 2.1 - PASSKIT / APPLE PAY ===

PassKit/Apple Pay is NOT actively used in this app. It appears only because we include the @stripe/stripe-react-native SDK for backend payment processing, which bundles Apple Pay capabilities by default. The merchantIdentifier "merchant.uk.locksafe.app" is configured solely to satisfy Stripe SDK initialization requirements.

What we actually use:
- Server-side Stripe payment intents only (created on backend)
- Traditional card processing on our web platform (NOT Apple Pay)
- No Apple Pay buttons, payment sheets, or PKPayment APIs anywhere in the app
- No calls to initPaymentSheet(), presentApplePay(), or confirmApplePayPayment()

If required, we can remove the PassKit entitlement in Build 9 and submit within 24 hours.

=== GUIDELINE 2.1(b) - BUSINESS MODEL ===

This is a B2B service provider app for professional locksmiths (comparable to Uber Driver, DoorDash Dasher, TaskRabbit Tasker). It is NOT a consumer app. There are NO in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid content.

1) WHO ARE THE USERS?
Professional, licensed UK locksmiths verified through our backend (license numbers, insurance). Locksmiths do NOT purchase anything within this app.

2) WHERE CAN USERS PURCHASE CONTENT/FEATURES?
Locksmiths DO NOT purchase anything. There are zero in-app purchases.

How the platform works:
- Customers visit www.locksafe.uk (web) and pay an assessment fee (£29-49) via Stripe
- The paid job appears in the locksmith's app
- Locksmith accepts job, performs the physical locksmith service on-site
- Locksmith receives earnings (85%) via Stripe Connect bank transfer
- LockSafe retains 15% commission (deducted before payout)

Zero financial transactions occur inside the mobile app.

3) WHAT PREVIOUSLY PURCHASED CONTENT CAN USERS ACCESS?
Not applicable - we do not provide "content". Locksmiths access JOB REQUESTS (work opportunities), not unlockable content. They view job details, customer location, and earnings history. Nothing was "purchased" by the locksmith; customers paid for a real-world locksmith service on the web platform.

4) WHAT PAID FEATURES ARE UNLOCKED WITHOUT IN-APP PURCHASE?
NONE. All app features are completely FREE to locksmiths:
- Job notifications, listings, acceptance/management
- GPS navigation, quote builder, photo documentation
- Digital signature capture, earnings tracker, job history

There are no premium tiers, subscriptions, feature unlocks, or content libraries.
