## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

- **Account & Profile Information:** Full name, email address, phone number, company name, and professional qualifications.
- **Identity Verification:** Documentation submitted during the partner application and vetting process.
- **Payment Information:** Bank account details and financial information required for Stripe Connect payouts.
- **Digital Signatures:** Customer signatures captured through the App for job completion verification.
- **Communications:** Messages, support requests, and feedback you send to us.

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** Real-time GPS location when you are set to "Online" or actively working on a job. This is used for job matching, route verification, and customer transparency.
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
- **Usage Data:** App interaction data including screens viewed, features used, timestamps, and session duration.
- **Push Notification Tokens:** Device tokens for delivering real-time job notifications.

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Stripe:** Transaction and payment status data from Stripe Connect.
- **Customer Feedback:** Ratings and reviews submitted by customers after job completion.

## 8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Request that we limit how we use your data.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **support@locksafe.uk**. We will respond within one calendar month.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)**:
- Website: https://ico.org.uk
- Phone: 0303 123 1113

## 9. Location Data

The App uses your device's GPS to:

- Match you with nearby job opportunities.
- Provide real-time location updates to customers while you are en route or on site.
- Verify job attendance for fraud prevention and dispute resolution.

Location tracking is active **only when you are set to "Online" or actively working on a job**. You can stop location tracking at any time by setting your availability to "Offline" or closing the App. You may also revoke location permissions through your device settings, though this may affect core app functionality.
