# Screen Recording Guide for Apple Review

## What to Record

Apple requires a screen recording showing:
1. Sign in with demo account (amiosif@icloud.com / demo1234)
2. Navigate to Settings tab
3. Scroll to "Delete Account" button
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. First confirmation dialog appears
6. Tap "Continue"
7. Second confirmation modal appears
8. Type "DELETE" in the text field
9. Tap "Delete Account" button
10. Account deletion confirmation
11. Redirect to login screen

## How to Record on iPhone

1. Open Control Center
2. Tap Screen Recording button
3. Start recording
4. Open LockSafe app
5. Follow the steps above
6. Stop recording

## Upload to Apple

1. Go to App Store Connect
2. Navigate to app version
3. Click "App Review Information"
4. Upload screen recording to "Notes" field
