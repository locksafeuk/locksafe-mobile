🔒 LockSafe Business Model Explained

Visual guide for App Store Review - No In-App Purchases Required

Payment & Service Flow

1
Customer
Visits www.locksafe.uk
Pays £29-49 assessment fee
ON WEB PLATFORM
(Not in mobile app)
→
2
Platform
Receives payment
Creates job request
Notifies nearby locksmiths
(Backend processing)
→
3
Locksmith
Sees job in MOBILE APP
Accepts job (free)
Performs physical service
Gets paid 75-85% via Stripe Connect

🎯 Critical Point

The mobile app is Step 3 only. By the time the locksmith sees the job in the app, the customer has already paid on the web platform. The locksmith uses the app for FREE to view jobs, navigate, and complete services. No money flows through the mobile app at any point.

What the App DOES vs DOESN'T Do

✅ What It DOES

  • Shows available job requests
  • GPS navigation to customer
  • Job status updates
  • Quote builder tool
  • Photo documentation
  • Digital signature capture
  • Earnings history (read-only)
  • All features FREE for locksmiths

❌ What It DOESN'T Do

  • Process payments
  • Sell subscriptions
  • Unlock paid features
  • Provide digital content
  • Use Apple Pay or In-App Purchase
  • Charge locksmiths for access
  • Require payment methods
  • Have premium tiers

📋 Key Facts for App Review

App Type

B2B Service Provider Platform (not consumer app)

Users

Professional locksmiths only (verified businesses)

Pricing

100% FREE for all locksmiths - no charges, subscriptions, or IAP

Revenue Model

Commission on services (15-25%) - exempt under Guideline 3.1.3(d)

Customer Payments

All processed on www.locksafe.uk web platform via Stripe

Locksmith Payouts

Stripe Connect direct to bank account (75-85% of job value)

PassKit/Apple Pay

Included via Stripe SDK but NOT implemented or used

Guidelines Compliance

Physical services performed outside app - IAP not required

✅ Why This Is Compliant

App Store Guideline 3.1.3(d): "Apps may use in-app purchase to sell and deliver physical goods or to purchase goods and services that will be consumed outside of the app."

LockSafe connects customers with locksmiths for PHYSICAL emergency locksmith services (lockouts, repairs, installations) performed AT THE CUSTOMER'S LOCATION. This is explicitly exempt from the In-App Purchase requirement, just like Uber rides, DoorDash deliveries, and TaskRabbit services.
